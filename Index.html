Retro-Future Lorenz Sandbox

This is an experimental, sandbox application developed using JavaScript. Stemming from the Lorenz Attractor system of ordinary differential equations, the z-axis of this three dimensional system dictates the color of the sandbox which ultimately gives it the "retro-futuristic" vibe. Check the boxes and slide the sliders. Simply do the thing and enjoy!
